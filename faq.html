Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ordering, delivery, and our fragrances.

Ordering

Browse our Shop page, open the product you'd like, and tap "Order via WhatsApp" or "Order via Email." Your message will already include the product name — just confirm the size you'd like and we'll handle the rest. There's no account or checkout to fill in.

No. We don't use accounts or online checkout. Every order happens through a direct WhatsApp or email conversation, so we can confirm details with you personally.

Payment is arranged directly with us once your order is confirmed — we'll share the available payment options when we chat on WhatsApp or email.

Delivery

We offer delivery within Kampala and can arrange delivery to other areas in Uganda on request. Exact delivery time and cost will be confirmed with you when you place your order, based on your location.

Most Kampala deliveries arrive within the same day or next day, depending on when your order is confirmed. We'll give you an estimated time when we confirm your order.

Our Products

Yes. Every fragrance we sell is sourced and quality-checked before it's listed. We're upfront about what each product is, including its fragrance family and notes, so there are no surprises.

We carry personal fragrances — perfume sprays, eau de parfum, eau de toilette, body oils, mists, splashes, roll-ons, hair perfumes, and gift sets — as well as home fragrances like reed diffusers, scented candles, room sprays, wax melts, and more. Browse the full range on our Shop page.

Longevity varies by product type and is listed on each product's detail page. Eau de parfums tend to last the longest (often 8+ hours), while body mists and splashes are lighter and meant for more frequent refreshing.

Returns & Exchanges

If you receive the wrong item or a product arrives damaged, message us within 48 hours of delivery and we'll arrange an exchange. Due to the nature of fragrance products, we're unable to accept returns on opened or used items unless there's a quality issue on our end.

Contact

The fastest way is WhatsApp — tap the button on any page. You can also email us, or use the contact form on our Contact page. We're happy to help you pick the right fragrance before you order.
